About Evergreen

A Local Houston Team Focused on High-Quality Outdoor Results

Evergreen Outdoor Design was built for homeowners who expect a higher level of planning, communication, and finish quality. We combine thoughtful design with disciplined field execution so your property looks exceptional and stays that way.

Our Story

We started Evergreen after seeing too many homeowners spend serious money on landscaping and still end up with generic results, missed schedules, and unclear communication.

Today, we serve high-end residential clients across Houston, Katy, Pearland, Sugar Land, Pasadena, and Cypress with a process built around planning, craftsmanship, and long-term property care.

What Makes Us Different

  • Design-Led Approach
  • Licensed & Insured
  • Reliable Scheduling
  • Clean Work Sites

We evaluate each property as a complete system: planting, hardscape, lighting, irrigation, drainage, and maintenance. That systems mindset is how we deliver cohesive results instead of disconnected one-off fixes.

FAQ

Answers to Common Landscaping Questions

How much does landscaping typically cost?

Costs depend on project size, materials, and service scope. Most projects begin with a consultation so we can provide a clear, itemized estimate aligned with your goals.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes. We offer weekly and seasonal maintenance options that can include mowing, edging, pruning, fertilization support, and cleanup visits.

Do you work on new-build homes?

Absolutely. We regularly design and install full landscapes for new construction, including irrigation, hardscaping, and lighting.

How often should lawns be serviced in Houston?

Most Houston lawns benefit from weekly service during peak growth, with adjustments by season. We recommend schedules based on your turf type.

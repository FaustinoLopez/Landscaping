Irrigation & Drainage

Water Management Solutions That Protect Your Landscape Investment

We design irrigation and drainage systems that deliver efficient watering, reduce runoff problems, and protect lawns, beds, and foundations during Houston rain events and long summer heat cycles.

Service Breakdown

From Sprinkler Optimization to Drainage Correction

Irrigation Zone Planning

We align watering schedules and coverage zones with plant types, sun exposure, and soil conditions for consistent, efficient moisture control.

Runoff & Pooling Solutions

Problem areas are corrected with practical flow routes and drainage components to prevent standing water, root stress, and hardscape wear.

System Performance Checks

Existing systems are reviewed for leaks, overwatering patterns, and inefficient coverage that drives water bills and damages plant health.

Benefits for Homeowners

  • Lower Water Waste
  • Healthier Turf & Beds
  • Reduced Water Damage Risk
  • Better Storm Resilience

Proper water control improves plant health and protects expensive outdoor upgrades such as hardscape, turf replacement, and foundation-adjacent zones.

Why Choose Evergreen

We treat water management as a critical part of landscape quality. Every recommendation is based on your lot layout and risk areas, not generic fixes.

Our team also coordinates drainage planning with landscape and hardscape work to avoid future conflicts and costly rework across systems.
